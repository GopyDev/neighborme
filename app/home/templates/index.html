about

We want to empower women to accomplish more, together.

Our Mission

Women today are busier than ever, yet often are hesitant to ask for help. As more and more people move away from their families, finding help isn't as easy as calling Grandma down the street.

Meanwhile, many women have vast and varied skills that go underutilized everyday. NeighborMe provides a platform to find and/or offer time, skills, or resources to those in need.

why only women?

We created NeighborMe to enable women to more easily harness the power of a nearby network. We believe carving out a female-focused space will help us build a stronger, safer community. 

With that said, we also want to support our male advocates, so we do not prevent anyone from joining on the basis of gender. We do ask all members to share how they identify in their profiles.
